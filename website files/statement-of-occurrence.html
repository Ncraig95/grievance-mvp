Statement of Occurrence Form

Use this form when: A member needs to document what happened so the union can review the facts and start the grievance workflow if appropriate.

What this form is for: The Statement of Occurrence captures the member's information, contract, supervisor, incident date, work location, statement, article involved, and witness information.

How to Fill It Out

  1. Choose the correct contract or work group.
  2. Enter the grievant's first name, last name, email, phone number, work location, and supervisor.
  3. Enter the incident date as accurately as possible.
  4. Write the statement in plain language. Include who was involved, what happened, when it happened, where it happened, and why it matters.
  5. Enter home address, seniority date, NCS date, department, title, supervisor contact information, UID, and article if known.
  6. Add witnesses if there are any. Include names, titles, and phone numbers when available.
  7. Use a personal email or reachable email for signing if the form asks for it.

What Should Happen After You Submit

The system sends the information into the grievance intake workflow, creates or updates the grievance record, generates the Statement of Occurrence document, and prepares the electronic signature step through DocuSeal when required.

Important: If the form does not open, does not submit, does not send you to the signing step, or any information looks wrong, contact the hall immediately.
