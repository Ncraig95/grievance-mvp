Mobility, BST, Bargaining, and Motion Forms

These forms support Mobility grievance records, BST or Utilities 3G3A workflows, Mobility bargaining suggestions, and motion sheets.

Important: If a form will not open, will not submit, sends a signer to the wrong email, or does not create the expected document, contact the hall immediately.

Mobility Record of Grievance

Use this form when: An AT&T Mobility grievance needs a formal record of grievance created for an existing grievance.

How to fill it out: Enter the grievance number, grievant name and email, district grievance number if known, occurrence date, department, location and state, employee or work group name, job title, NCS date, union statement, contract articles, informal date, first step request and held dates, and all required signer emails.

What it should do: The system generates the Mobility Record of Grievance and routes it through the staged signature workflow.

BST Grievance Form 3G3A

Use this form when: A BST or Utilities grievance needs the 3G3A grievance form prepared.

How to fill it out: Enter the existing grievance number, contract, request summary, occurrence date, city and state, employee or work group name, ATTUID, department, job title, payroll ID, seniority date, job title requested or involved, requisition number if applicable, other department if applicable, union statement, contract basis, informal meeting date, 3G3R issued date, union representative name and ATTUID, and all required signer emails.

What it should do: The system generates the BST 3G3A form and routes it through the staged signature workflow.

CWA 3106 - 3G3A Extension Request

Use this form when: A BST or Utilities grievance needs a 3G3A extension request.

How to fill it out: Enter the grievance number, contract, request summary, occurrence date, city and state, employee or work group name, ATTUID if known, department, job title, payroll ID, seniority date, job title requested or involved, requisition number if applicable, union statement, contract basis, informal meeting date, 3G3R issued date, union representative name and ATTUID, and all required signer emails.

What it should do: The system generates the 3G3A extension request and routes it through the staged signature workflow.

AT&T Mobility Bargaining Suggestion

Use this form when: A member or local wants to submit an AT&T Mobility bargaining demand or suggestion.

How to fill it out: Enter the local number, demand from local, submitting member title and name, demand text, reason, specific examples, work phone, home phone, non-work email, and local president signer email if an override is needed.

What it should do: The system creates the bargaining suggestion document, sends it for the required signature when configured, and files the completed document in the standalone workflow.

Motion Sheet

Use this form when: A motion needs to be captured for union records.

How to fill it out: Enter who made the motion and the exact motion text.

What it should do: The system generates the motion sheet and files it in the Motion Sheets folder for printing or records.
