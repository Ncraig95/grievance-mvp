CWA Local 3106 Referral Form

Use this form when: A member wants to refer another worker to CWA Local 3106 for follow-up.

What this form is for: The referral form collects the referrer's contact information, the referred person's name, company or group, AT&T UID if known, and notes for the hall.

How to Fill It Out

  1. Enter your full name, phone number, address, and email if you have one.
  2. Select or type your company or work group.
  3. Enter the full name of the person you are referring.
  4. Enter the referred person's company or work group.
  5. Enter the referred person's AT&T UID if you know it. Leave it blank if you do not know it.
  6. Use the notes box for anything the hall should know before contacting the person.

What Should Happen After You Submit

The system records the referral and routes it for officer follow-up. The hall can use the information to contact the referred worker and track the referral.

Important: If the form does not open, does not submit, shows an error, or you do not think the referral went through, contact the hall immediately.
